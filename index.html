About Raul

After graduating from UW Parkside with a degree in Computer Science, I opened a trucking company as the sole owner. This was a new and challenging experience, however, it did not feel like the right fit for me. After all, the reason I had even chosen computer science as my major was the result of the joy I felt creating my first programs in Qbasic back in high school. The feeling of accomplishment I got after finally getting the computer to do what I wanted was great!

If you are looking for a new addition to your developer team, please keep me in mind. Although my research internship was a collaboration between 3 people, I also have experience in an agile development setting using SCRUM from a university final project. I recall how exciting the group projects were and can not wait to repeat the experience of building great software together!
